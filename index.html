a letter for you

Dear Asjad Mama, Since day one, you have always cared for the four of us. Even when you were still so young yourself, and despite all the difficulties you had faced in your own life, you always made sure that we never had to go through any of those hardships. You have always done your best for us, guided us, adviced us, providing for us, been there for us, supported us and made time to create fun memories with us, whether it was watching harry potter movies together in 2015, or death note in 2025, spending time with us, laughing with us, every time, it meant the most. It’s unbelieavable to us how, at our age now, you were already carrying so much responsibility & dilligence to be the best, which you have rightfully achieved now. Although we don’t always listen to you, and might have disappointed you at times, please know that it was never because we valued you any less. Sometimes we are just stubborn, but your words, concern, and efforts will always stay with us. We notice, appreciate & carry it with us. and we hope to make you proud one day as you make us proud everyday. We truly pray that Allah SWT blesses you with every happiness possible, fills your life with peace, success, good health, wealth & immense Barakah. May He fulfil all your prayers, reward you for everything, every sacrifice, every act of care, and every time you chose to be there for us. Thank you for being such a wonderful mama, for being our guide, our support, our mentor and someone we can always look up to. We are truly blessed to have you in our lives.

— sidra, maryam, maarya & mubashshira
